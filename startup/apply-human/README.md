# The Problem
AI has made job applications easier, but it has also made everyone sound the same. Recruiters and founders are being flooded with generic, "ChatGPT-style" messages that lack personality and specificity, leading to lower reply rates.

# The Solution
**ApplyHuman** (initially conceived as VoiceMail AI) rewrites AI-generated drafts or writes new messages from scratch using a "Voice DNA" profile. It creates messages that sound like the real sender, tailored specifically to the job post and the sender's actual communication style (sentence length, confidence, vocabulary).

# Market Research
- **Target Audience:** Job seekers (especially for startup roles), founders doing outreach, freelancers.
- **Potential Competitors:** Sinceerly, Gmail AI tools, generic AI writing assistants.
- **Unique Selling Point (USP):** "Voice DNA" personalization (learning from past emails/posts) and specialized modes like "CEO Mode" or "Founder Mode" to maximize reply probability.

# Business Model
- **Free:** 5 rewrites/month.
- **Pro ($5/mo):** Unlimited rewrites, saved voice profile, job-specific messages.
- **Power ($19/mo):** Chrome extension, LinkedIn/Wellfound support, outreach tracking.

# Core Features
1. **Personal Voice Profile (Voice DNA):** Connect past emails, LinkedIn, or GitHub to learn your style.
2. **CEO/Founder Modes:** Short, sharp, and confident output modes.
3. **“Will they reply?” Score:** Clarity, cringe, and AI-sounding scores before sending.
4. **One-click Job App Generator:** Detects job pages (Wellfound, LinkedIn) and creates tailored messages.
