# 1. Vision

**Unistory** is a **universal storytelling and content platform** that transforms raw content (stories, knowledge, media) into **engaging, structured, and monetizable story formats**—powered by AI.

### Core Idea:
> Everything on the internet can be turned into a story.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Today’s content ecosystem is broken:
* Content is **scattered and unstructured**
* Blogs are boring, long, and low-retention
* Short-form content lacks depth
* Creators struggle to:
  * Retain attention
  * Monetize effectively
* Users want:
  * Fast + engaging + meaningful content

---

# 3. Solution

Unistory creates a **story-first content layer** over the internet:
* Converts content into:
  * Stories
  * Episodes
  * Series
* Makes content:
  * Engaging
  * Binge-worthy
  * Structured

---
